WHO WE ARE

Hello and welcome! My name is Pat, owner and founder of Pat's Salmon Cookies. We are a homegrown Seattle family business who strive to create a unique culinary experience for visitors and locals alike! Our cookies are loved by all who try them and for good reason, we are very passionate about what we do and it shows in the quality of our product. We currently have 5 locations to serve you wherever you may be when you get the craving for some Pat's Salmon Cookies, even more to come soon.

THE PRODUCT

We offer a truly unique product that makes our customers come back time and time again for more! We start by taking only the freshest salmon (yes, REAL salmon!) and infusing it into a perfectly baked cookie delight that pairs perfectly with another Seattle staple, coffee! Enjoy them during coffee breaks, as a snack, or even a late night desert, no matter what time of the day it is, a Pat's Salmon Cookie™ will satisfy your cravings.
